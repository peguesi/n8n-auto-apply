About

Hi, I’m Isaiah Pegues — I build smart products with fewer resources and more creativity.


Founder, product leader, and unapologetic problem solver with a focus on AI, SaaS, and automation. I’ve spent the last few years turning complex workflows into intuitive tools—often without a CTO, a big team, or the luxury of waiting around.


Whether it’s training AI models on synthetic data, hacking together features in hours, or scaling platforms to thousands of users, I believe in shipping fast, simplifying the messy stuff, and letting technology earn its keep.


I’m passionate about building products where AI isn’t just a feature—it’s the teammate that gets things done.
